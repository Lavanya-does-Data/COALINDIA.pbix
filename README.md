# COALINDIA.pbix
This COALINDIA business Power BI project depicts area graph sum of volume & count of date by turnover in lacks along with two cards showing sum of high & sum of low & a slicer for date; a line graph showing sum of low by high; bar graph showing sum of number of trades by year.
Data source: Kaggle.
